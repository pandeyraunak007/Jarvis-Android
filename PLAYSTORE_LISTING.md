## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Voxn AI is your all-in-one personal life management dashboard. Track your spending, build habits, monitor health metrics, and manage notes — all from a sleek, futuristic interface designed to keep you in control.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

📊 SMART SPENDING TRACKER
• Log expenses and income with one tap
• Auto-categorize transactions from bank SMS notifications
• 70+ Indian merchants recognized (Swiggy, Zomato, Amazon, Uber, and more)
• Set monthly budgets and savings goals with visual progress tracking
• 14-day spending charts, week-over-week trends, and category breakdown
• Add recurring monthly bills that auto-log on schedule
• Filter by category, search by merchant, navigate by month

🏃 HEALTH CONNECT INTEGRATION
• View steps, calories, sleep, and workout data from Health Connect
• Beautiful arc gauges show daily progress toward your goals
• 7-day weekly trend charts for every metric
• Detailed readouts with daily averages
• Read-only — your health data never leaves your device

✅ HABIT TRACKER
• Create daily habits with customizable reminders
• Track completion with a satisfying tap
• View current streaks with flame badges
• Monthly completion calendar to visualize consistency
• Smart notifications with Done and Snooze actions — complete habits right from the notification

📝 NOTES & REMINDERS
• Create notes with categories (Personal, Office) and priorities (High, Medium, Low)
• Set due dates and one-time reminders
• Pin important notes to the top
• Mark tasks as complete
• Filter by category, priority, or search text
• Notification actions: Complete, Snooze 15m, Snooze 30m

🖥️ DASHBOARD HUD
• Daily overview of all your data in one glance
• Time-based greetings and smart date display
• Today's spending, weekly and monthly totals
• Top expense categories at a glance
• Health gauges for steps, calories, sleep, workout
• Habit completion rings with streaks

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

DESIGNED FOR PRIVACY
• All data stored locally on your device
• No accounts, no cloud sync, no analytics
• No ads, no tracking, no data collection
• Health Connect used in read-only mode
• Clear app data or uninstall to delete everything

BUILT FOR INDIA
• INR (₹) currency throughout
• 70+ Indian merchant auto-categorization
• UPI, credit card, debit card, wallet & cash payment methods
• Works offline — no internet required

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Voxn AI is free, private, and built to help you take control of your daily life. No subscriptions. No hidden costs. Just a clean, powerful dashboard for your life.
